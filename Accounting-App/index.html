Welcome to Quick-Accounts!

Quick-Accounts is a simple to use spreadsheet program. It allows you to add or delete entries and see the transaction activity displayed as charts.

Instructions for use:

  • Enter details into the transcation form at the bottom of the screen.
  • Press 'add' to enter the transaction into the ledger.
  • Furthur transction entries will produce a runnung balance.
  • Entries may be deleted by pressing the cross next to the entry. The running balance will be re-calculated automatically.
  • Selecting 'Charts' on either the side or top bars will display the balance in graphical form with the dates shown chronologically.
  • To the right of the Charts page is a 'Show Incoming/Outgoing' button. Pressing this will display a bar chart showing deposits and withdrawals vertically.
  • It is possible to reverse the order of the dates shown by going to the Settings page and selecting 'Reverse Date Order'.
